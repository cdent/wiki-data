Retrieve Wiki-Data File

The Avox Wiki-Data files are available to subscribers using an FTP connection to the Avox FTP server: ftp1.avox.info

Once you have received your FTP Username and Password you can view and copy the Wiki-Data files that are currently available.

You can use one of several tools to access these files. To assist we have prepared a Guide that covers the essential information required for access to the files. This goes into detail for two of the most popular FTP free clients on Microsoft Windows, Filezilla and WinSCP.

If you require any assistance please email wiki-data@avox.info or contact us at +44 1978 661 813 during UK business hours.
